MedPath – AI Ward & Clinical Companion

1. Project Overview

MedPath – AI Ward & Clinical Companion is an AI-powered web application developed for MBBS students to support their learning during clinical rotations and self-study. The platform brings together disease information, drug references, investigation interpretation, clinical notes, and an AI assistant into one easy-to-use application. It is designed to reduce the time students spend searching through multiple books and websites and provide quick, organized medical information.


---

2. Problem Statement

Medical students often use many different resources to study diseases, drugs, laboratory investigations, and patient management. This process is time-consuming and can interrupt learning during ward rotations. Students also need a simple platform where they can revise clinical topics, organize notes, and receive AI-assisted guidance.


---

3. Solution

MedPath solves this problem by providing a single platform that combines important clinical learning tools. Students can search diseases, read drug information, interpret laboratory investigations, save personal notes, and interact with an AI Clinical Assistant. The application is designed to be simple, mobile-friendly, and useful for daily medical learning.

5. AI Feature

The AI Clinical Assistant is designed to help MBBS students understand medical topics in a structured way.

The assistant provides information using the following format whenever possible:

Definition

Causes

Risk Factors

Symptoms

Differential Diagnosis

History Questions

Physical Examination

Investigations

Management

Drug Treatment

Clinical Pearls

Red Flags

Patient Education


The project is designed to integrate with the Google Gemini API for AI-generated responses.
